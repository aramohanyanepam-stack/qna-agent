Q: What is the QnA-Agent?
A: The QnA-Agent is a smart question-answering agent that can retrieve information from a knowledge base.

Q: How do I ask a question?
A: You can send a POST request to the /api/v1/chat/sessions/{session_id}/messages/ endpoint with your question in the message body.

Q: Can I use it with Docker?
A: Yes, the QnA-Agent comes with a Dockerfile and a docker-compose.yml file for easy deployment.
